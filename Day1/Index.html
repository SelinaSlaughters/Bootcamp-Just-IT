Software Development Bootcamp

Participants are currently enrolled onto the Government funded Software Development Bootcamp with Just IT. The Bootcamp is held virtually and runs from Monday to Friday, from 09:00 - 12:30 for the next three months. Upon completion of the Bootcamp, participants can expect to have developed relevent skills and completed a Developer portfolio which can be presented to future employers.

Career

There are a number of career paths for participants to explore after the 12 weeks. Sumatosoft states, "The software development area is constantly changing. Every few months, new areas of expertise, technology, and methodology appear, which are forged by the unremitting innovation of the software industry. Consequently, new types of software developer jobs and careers appear." See a list of developer jobs below:

  2. Web Developer
